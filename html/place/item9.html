Nameless#1

In this series of paintings, my thoughts on the meaning of life and spiritual stages in a person's life are demonstrated. Each of us enters a certain stage of life when questions arise not only about what to wear or eat, but about the bigger picture and the meaning of the individual in life. I am talking about such questions as: what am I for, what is my meaning, what will I leave behind, what do I believe in, and many other spiritual questions.
